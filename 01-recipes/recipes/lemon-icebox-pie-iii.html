Lemon Icebox Pie III

Description

A family favorite when a no-bake, fast pie is needed. (And family members too small to use the oven can make dessert!) Very pretty when garnished with whipped cream and mint leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crus
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1 teaspon lemon zest

Steps

  • In a medium mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy
  • Add condensed milk, lemon juice, and lemon rind. Mix until smooth.
  • Pour mixture into crust.
  • Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
  • Garnis with whipped cream and mint leaves if desired.
