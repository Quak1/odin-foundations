Easy Air Fried Corn Ribs

Description

A family favorite when a no-bake, fast pie is needed. (And family members too small to use the oven can make dessert!) Very pretty when garnished with whipped cream and mint leaves.

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of corn on the cob
  • 3 tablespoons "Everything but the Elote seasoningo"
  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 1/4 cup grated cotija cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Mexican crema, crema fresca
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh cilantro, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 200 degrees C for 7 minutes
  2. Meanwhile stand corn cobs on the end, and with a sharp knife cut each ear down the middle. place corn cob halves flat on a cutting board and cut in half lengthwise.
  3. Air fry corb ribs in batch for a total of 12 minutes. Remove from air fryer.
  4. Drizzle crema con sal over corn 'ribs' and sprinkle with cotija cheese and remaining seasoning. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
